Milange Province ‒ Mozambique

In person event participants at the White House will contribute to OpenStreetMap for the district of Milange, Mozambique as part of the President’s Malaria Initiative Strategy and in conjunction with the US Agency for International Development (USAID), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and the Peace Corps.
