Calling All Citizen Mappers

White House Mapathon

An event to celebrate and actively participate in open mapping for the Sustainable Development Goals

July 7th, 2016 #WHMaps

Get Started Mapping

Map with Us


Please join the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy in illuminating how open mapping can help achieve the sustainable development goals. We are calling on citizen mappers around the world to host or participate in mapathons in conjunction with the 2016 White House Mapathon.

On July 7, 2016, citizen mappers will make at least 25,000 open mapping edits at the White House Mapathon. It’s up to you how many edits are made worldwide.

  • Register a Satellite Event
    • Host a satellite event. Your event doesn’t have to be at the exact same time as the White House event. We encourage you to schedule a time and place that works for you. Invite your local citizen cartographer friends, and register your project.
